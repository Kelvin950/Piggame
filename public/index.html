PIG GAME

How to play

How do I send invites?

Send an invite by cliking on a players name.

How do I accept an invite?

Click on accept invite. Game starts immediately.

How do I roll the dice?

Click on roll dice to roll the dice.

What does the hold button do?

Current player can click the hold button to save current score to main score. The current player is switched after that.

Can I start a new game in the middle of the game ?

NO, a new game can only be started when a player wins the game.

How do I win then?

A player can only win when the players main score is 100

What are the rules of the game

  • The player who sends the invite is the first player.
  • A player can roll the dice as long as the dice does not return a one
  • When a one is returned player losses current score and control is switched to second player ie the players controls are disabled
  • Current players side is always highlighted with bold text.
